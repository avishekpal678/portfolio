Study of Electric Locomotives

Completed a four-week industrial training at Chittaranjan Locomotive Works (CLW), where I studied the design, manufacturing, and operation of modern electric locomotives such as WAP-7 and WAG-9. Gained practical exposure to traction motors, propulsion systems, braking systems, bogies, high-voltage equipment, and locomotive assembly processes. The training enhanced my understanding of how mechanical, electrical, and electronic systems are integrated to deliver safe, reliable, and high-performance railway operations.

Kinematic Analysis and Simulation of 3-RRR Planar Parallel Manipulator

Conducted a detailed study and simulation of a 3-RRR planar parallel manipulator, focusing on its kinematic behavior, motion analysis, and singularity identification. Developed simulations to visualize platform movement and evaluate system performance under different configurations. The project strengthened my understanding of parallel robotics, mathematical modeling, and mechanism design while highlighting the importance of singularity analysis for stable and reliable operation.

Assistive device for people with limited mobility

Designed and developed a low-cost medical assistance device to safely transfer paralyzed patients between wheelchairs and vehicles. Created complete 3D CAD models and assemblies in SolidWorks, incorporating a manually operated hydraulic lifting mechanism for height adjustment and stability. Performed material selection, component design, and engineering analysis, focusing on affordability, structural reliability, patient comfort, and caregiver safety.
